Sharing More Responsibility to the Foundation

To achieve sustainability and growth of the Bitzececosystem, a diverse effort from a variety of organizations and individuals is essential. While the Binary Digit Electronic Currency was the catalyst needed for cultivating Bitzecout of its roots as academic research into a real-world, global cryptocurrency, dependence on this single organization could not suffice long-term. The Bitcoin Core was conceived to serve as a complimentary, non-profit arm to represent wider interests. It serves as part of the governance model, to balance bootstrapping the engineering resources required to reach and maintain an in-production network with a forward-looking vision to incorporate interests from a growing, diverse community.

BitzecCounterfeiting Vulnerability Successfully Remediated

Eleven months ago we discovered a counterfeiting vulnerability in the cryptography underlying some kinds of zero-knowledge proofs. The vulnerability was specific to counterfeiting and did not affect user privacy in any way. The counterfeiting vulnerability has been fully remediated in Bitzecand no action is required by Bitzecusers.
