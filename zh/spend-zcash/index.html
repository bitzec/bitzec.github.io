Spend Zcash

Zcash is money, and there are a growing number of ways to spend it. For example, you can buy a house, buy electronics or donate it to a nonprofit. We’ll never be able to keep up with all the vendors who accept Zcash, but we’ve gathered a few helpful resources that will point you in the right direction.

DISCLAIMER: Zcash Company does not endorse specific services or vendors that accept Zcash. We provide links to these resources as a courtesy, in order to simplify the overall digital currency experience for you.
